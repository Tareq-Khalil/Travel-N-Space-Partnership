Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a strategic partnership between STEM October Astronomy Club (SOAC), a youth-led astronomy initiative in Egypt, and Travel N Space (TNST), the UAE's leading provider of mobile astronomy and space experiences.

Partnership Vision

By combining SOAC's passion-driven grassroots community with TNST's professional expertise and innovative space offerings, this partnership aims to expand astronomy outreach in Egypt and the region.

What We'll Create Together:

  • Unique stargazing experiences
  • Educational workshops and courses
  • International collaborations
  • Cross-border astronomical events

Together, we envision making astronomy accessible, inspiring, and engaging for students, families, and enthusiasts across borders.

About STEM October Astronomy Club

Student-Led Excellence in Astronomy Education

Our Mission

We are STEM October Astronomy Club (SOAC), a high-school student-led astronomy initiative based in Egypt. Our mission is to spark curiosity, inspire young learners, and make astronomy accessible through education, events, and practical exploration.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Student Leadership: Founded and led entirely by passionate students
  • International Recognition: Members recognized in international Olympiads
  • Practical Experience: Hands-on workshops and real astronomical observations
  • Educational Focus: Delivering structured astronomy courses within our community

Notable Achievement: First Arab student to win a silver medal at the Open World Astronomy Olympiad in Russia

Our Key Achievements

Our Vision for Partnership

Synergy Between Passion and Professionalism
